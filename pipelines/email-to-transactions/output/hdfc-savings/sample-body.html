To validate Digital Certificate in your PDF statement, follow below steps.
1. Right click on Signature and then click on Validate Signature
2. Next, click on Signature Properties
3. Click on 'Show Signer's Certificate'
4. Then, click on 'Trust' tab
5. Click on 'Add to Trusted Certificates...' Then click on Ok.
6. Next, click on Check box of 'Certified documents' and enable all the below check box. Then click on Ok.
7. Click on 'Validate Signature'
