Hi there,

Kuvera is entering a new chapter in its journey. And we're glad
to have you with us.

Earlier in 2024, CRED and Kuvera signed definitive agreements for
the acquisition of Arevuk Advisory Services Private Limited
(AASPL) – the company behind the Kuvera app and kuvera.in 
( https://app.kuvera.in ).
With SEBI's formal approval now in place, this transition is
entering its final stage.

What does this mean for you?

In one word: progress.

You can expect more powerful features, faster innovation, and the
same Kuvera experience you've come to trust, just stronger, with
CRED's backing.

Your investments, your data, and your Kuvera access remains
secure, private and uninterrupted. No changes are needed from
your end. In the coming months, you may notice some backend
updates, including a change in the Kuvera app publisher on the
App Store and Play Store to CRED.

Everything else – your dashboard, your goals, your plans – remain
exactly where you left them.

At Kuvera, simplicity, transparency, and user-first design aren't
features, they're fundamentals. This doesn't change.

What does: the scale and speed at which we'll now be able to
serve you.

For any questions, we're here at support@kuvera.in 
( https://app.kuvera.in/support )

Thank you for being a part of Kuvera's story. The best chapters
are still ahead.

warm regards,
