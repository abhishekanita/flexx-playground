Ace: "Seedha bolunga — SEBI ki 2023 study says 89% F&O traders lost money. Average loss ₹1.1L per person. Ye casino nahi hai — ye worse than casino hai kyunki casino mein tujhe pata hai odds against hain.

₹2L agar 12% CAGR pe invest kare toh 5 saal mein ₹3.5L hoga. F&O mein 89% chance hai ki ₹2L become ₹0 or worse in 3 months.

Agar genuinely market mein active rehna hai — individual stocks dekh. Research kar, 3-year horizon rakh, aur ₹2L ko 4-5 stocks mein diversify kar. Woh bhi risky hai, but at least equity own karega.

F&O ke liye — jab tak ₹50L+ liquid portfolio nahi hai aur 2+ years trading experience nahi hai, door reh."
