Statement of Financial Transactions - SFT (for IT Return filing).
This statement provides complete information submitted to CBDT (Central Bureau of Direct Taxes) on Mutual Fund transactions and transfers for computation of capital gain /loss and display in the Annual Information Statement (AIS) (Form 26AS) and Pre-filled ITR. This includes Capital gains on transfer of units, wherever applicable.

The securities are classified for determining the type of asset viz. Equity and Non- Equity. The period of holding (difference between date of sale and date of acquisition of any security) is used to classify the asset as short term or long-term asset. First in First out (FIFO) method is used for identification of corresponding purchases and computation of period of holding.
